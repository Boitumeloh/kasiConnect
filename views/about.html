Who We Are

The Kasi Event App is a platform dedicated to promoting events and services in the township economy. Our goal is to connect local communities with the best entertainment, businesses, and services available.

Our Mission

We aim to create a central hub where township residents and visitors can easily discover events, book services, and explore the rich cultural heritage of their local community.

Our Vision

We envision a thriving township economy that fosters growth and opportunity for local businesses, entertainers, and service providers. Our platform is designed to empower local entrepreneurs and bring greater exposure to their offerings.
